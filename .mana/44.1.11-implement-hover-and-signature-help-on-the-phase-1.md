## Goal
Add point-of-use semantic explanation surfaces on top of the shared read-oriented code-intelligence contract.

## Current state
- The plan doc places `hover` and `signature help` in Phase 1 after diagnostics/definition/references and symbols.
- These surfaces are narrower than diagnostics and references but important for understanding meaning and callsites without jumping away.

## Scope
- `hover`
- `signature help`
- normalized content shaping for docs/signatures/ranges
- compact transcript summaries

## Acceptance
- The runtime can answer symbol-meaning and active-call-signature questions without raw protocol leakage.
- Results compose cleanly with `go-to-definition`, `find references`, and symbol navigation.
- The shared runtime contract stays stable even if backend details differ by language.
